Quotation: "The use of a pre-exisiting phrase, sentence, or paragraph which is taken from another source"
"The Lord Roars from Zion..."
 Am 1:2 and Joel 4:16
Allusion: "One or more words whose appearance intends to elicit the reader's recollection of another text (or texts) for a specific purpose" p.109
Build houses and inhabit them...
 Jr 29:4-5 and Is 65:18-19a, 21-22
Catchwords: "A type of allusion by using/reusing significant words to to refer to another text(s)"
Cup of Wrath
Themes and Motifs: Literary devices which a sophisitcated author can embed into a text for a sophisticated reader to "recognize, analyze, and assimilte" in order to convey meaning.
Desolation of Cities
